About · devx labs

An AI-native build partner for consumer brands.

We design like a top-tier consulting firm that ships code. The what, the why, and the how — before the portfolio mapping.

What

We build AI-native customer experiences for consumer brands.

devx labs designs and ships the customer-facing AI that consumer brands compete on — conversational commerce, personalization, headless storefronts — and the operations and architecture underneath that let it scale. AI is the design principle, not a feature bolted on afterward.

The work spans the full journey: web, app, in-store and WhatsApp, unified into one experience. From AR-led skin analysis to sub-two-second storefronts, it is built and in production today.

Why

Consumer brands compete on experience and margin at once.

Those two pressures usually pull against each other. AI is where they resolve — sharper discovery and conversion on one side, fewer operational fire-drills and lower cost to serve on the other.

For an investor, the read is portfolio-level: the same class of AI investment lifts revenue quality and operational efficiency across many brands at once, rather than one bespoke build at a time.

How

Three layers. One anchor.

We lead with Customer Experience — our core strength — compound it with AI-Led Operations, and stand both on a solid Enterprise Architecture foundation. Most engagements touch more than one layer.

Every use case in the mapping is tied to something already delivered, so it reads as a menu of proven moves rather than a wishlist.
